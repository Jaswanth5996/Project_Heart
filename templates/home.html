Helping you differentiate between gastric pain and heart issues with precision and peace of mind.

Discover the Device

The Problem

Many patients struggle to differentiate between gastric pain and heart issues, which often leads to delayed medical attention, heart damage, and even heart attacks. Accurate diagnosis of these symptoms is critical for timely treatment and effective care.

Use Case

Initial Diagnosis

For patients who face overlapping symptoms of heart and gastric pain before visiting the doctor, Project Heart provides a quick and accurate diagnosis, distinguishing between these two conditions.

Post-Visit Remote Monitoring

Even after visiting the doctor, patients can rely on Project Heart for continuous monitoring and accurate updates about the health of their heart and stomach, all from the comfort of their home.

The Solution: Project Heart

How the Device Works

Our biocompatible medical device attaches securely to the skin, providing accurate heart and gastric health updates.

  • Placement: The central portion of the device is positioned near the diaphragm. A heart sensor is placed on the heart, and a gastric sensor is attached to the stomach.
  • Monitoring: The device monitors both heart and gastric activity for 5 minutes, after which it is detached from the body.
  • Health Update: The deviceâ€™s screen provides instant updates on heart and gastric health. A QR code is also generated for detailed reports.

Customer Value Proposition

Project Heart is patient-independent. It enables individuals to determine the severity level of heart or gastric issues without needing prior medical knowledge. The device keeps them informed about their current heart and gastric health status, providing peace of mind and timely intervention.
